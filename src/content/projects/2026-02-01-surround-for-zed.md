A Zed editor extension that brings the `vscode-surround` experience to Zed. Select text, trigger the command, and wrap it with your choice of snippet — brackets, tags, functions, and more.

Unfortunately, this package was never approved by the Zed maintainers, so it actually hasn't been published to the extension store. But you can download and run this locally and use it from your local machine.
