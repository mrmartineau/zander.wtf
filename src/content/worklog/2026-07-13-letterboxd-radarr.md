Added a **Radarr-compatible Letterboxd watchlist endpoint** to [my-api-tools](https://github.com/mrmartineau/my-api-tools), my growing collection of personal APIs running on a Cloudflare Worker. It scrapes my Letterboxd watchlist and serves it in a format Radarr understands as an import list — add a film on Letterboxd, and it shows up ready to grab.
